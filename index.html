Fitur Utama

Scan Cepat

Pindai dokumen fisik apa pun seperti struk, kontrak, atau memo secara instan menggunakan kamera perangkat Anda.

PDF Berkualitas Tinggi

Konversikan dan simpan hasil pemindaian langsung ke format PDF dengan kualitas tulisan dan gambar yang sangat tajam.

Kelola Dokumen

Organisasikan dan simpan semua berkas pindaian Anda dengan aman di dalam satu repositori penyimpanan lokal.

Pencarian Instan

Temukan dokumen yang Anda cari dalam sekejap berkat fitur pencarian judul dokumen yang efisien.

Bagikan Mudah

Kirimkan file PDF Anda secara instan ke email, aplikasi pengolah pesan, atau penyimpanan awan (cloud).

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Easy Scan Document ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

Information We Collect

Easy Scan Document may collect:

  • Documents and images selected or scanned by the user.
  • Diagnostic and crash report information for system stability.
  • Advertising-related identifiers provided by third-party advertising services (e.g., Google AdMob).

How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Provide core app functionality (local scanning, crop filters, and exporting PDF files).
  • Improve overall app performance, resolve crashes, and enhance UI stability.
  • Display non-intrusive advertisements to users.
  • Respond to support queries and feedback tickets.

Advertising

This application may display advertisements through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob. These providers may collect, target, and process data (such as advertising IDs) according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

All scanned documents and generated PDF files are stored strictly locally on the user's iOS device. We do not host, store, or transmit your documents to external servers.

Third-Party Services

This application may use:

  • Google AdMob
  • Apple Core Services

These services collect information in accordance with their respective privacy policies.
